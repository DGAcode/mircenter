FATIMA & LISBON

DAY 1 DEPART USA

Our pilgrimage begins as we depart on our transatlantic flight to Lisbon, Portugal. Enjoy dinner and breakfast on board your overnight flight.

DAY 2 LISBON (D)

Afternoon arrival. Transfer to your hotel. Dinner and overnight.

DAY 3 LISBON (B & D)

Morning tour of Lisbon. Holy Mass at the Church of St. Anthony. Contrary of popular believe, St, Anthony was born in Lisbon. Prayers to St. Anthony are considered the best way to find lost objects. Dinner and overnight in Lisbon

DAY 4 LISBON – SANTAREM – FATIMA (B & D)

Morning departure to Fatima. Enroute, stop in Santarem (one of the oldest cities in the world) to visit the Eucharistic Miracle located in the Church of St. Stephen’s (in display since 1929, the oldest in the world). We will celebrate Holy Mass there. Continue then to Fatima. Dinner and overnight.

DAY 5 - 7 FATIMA ( B & D)

Your tour guide will inform you of your day by day itinerary upon arrival. By the time your stay in Fatima is over, you would have seen all of Fatima and experienced the spirituality and beauty of this town. You will visit the Basilica, the site where Francisco, Jacinta, and Lucia’s graves are located, the hamlet of Aljustrel, where the children lived, and Valinhos, the site of Our Lady’s apparition after the children’s return from prison. Follow the Stations of The Cross along “the Sacred Way” culminating in the Chapel of Calvary, which overlooks all of Fatima. In the evenings, you will join pilgrims from around the world in an evening candlelight procession to pray the rosary (requested by Our Lady). Bring your white handkerchief for the “Adeus” farewell. Your prayers will join those of pilgrims from all over the world as you pray for the sick, and drink water for the miraculous well. You will have the opportunity to attend the blessing of the sick. You will also have some free time for personal activities. Breakfast and dinner daily.

DAY 8 FATIMA – USA (B)

Early morning transfer to Lisbon Airport for our return flight home.
